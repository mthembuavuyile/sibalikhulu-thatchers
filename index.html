About Us

We Build More Than Roofs

Sibalikhulu Thatchers brings together time-honoured thatching traditions and modern construction standards. Based in KwaZulu-Natal, we serve residential homeowners, lodges, resorts, and commercial estates across South Africa.

Every project — from a single lapa to a full lodge complex — receives the same meticulous attention to structural integrity, aesthetic finish, and long-term durability.
